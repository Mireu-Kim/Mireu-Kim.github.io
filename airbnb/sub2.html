Located at the heart of Seoul, STAY256 is a century old Korean traditional housing, Hanok. STAY256 offers antique rooms with traditional furniture. The courtyard is a beautiful place where you can relax and chill, or have some fun with other guests. At STAY256, you can enjoy tea in a traditional chinaware, in a Korean traditional style living room, while a flatscreen TV with cable channels is readily available. We will try our best for your comfortable stay, so please feel free to contact us.

서울의 중심부에 위치한 STAY256은 한 세기 된 한옥이다. STAY256은 전통적인 가구가 딸린 골동품 방을 제공한다. 안뜰은 휴식을 취하며 더위를 식히거나 다른 손님들과 함께 즐거운 시간을 보낼 수 있는 아름다운 곳이다. STAY256에서는 전통 도자기, 한국식 거실에서 차를 즐길 수 있고, 케이블 채널이 있는 평면 TV도 쉽게 구할 수 있다. 편안한 숙박을 위해 최선을 다 할 테니 언제든지 연락해.

지역정보

Hyun님의 숙소는 종로구 서울 한국에 있습니다.

STAY256 is located only minutes walk away from tourist attractions such as Gyeongbokgung palace, Bukchon Hanok village, Insa-dong and many more. Sinchon, Itaewon and Gangnam are only a few kilometers away, so they are easily reachable by subway, bus or taxi.

교통편

Anguk subway station(line3) is one kilometer away, and Gwanghwamun station(line5) is 1.5 kilometer away. both can be reached by bus or taxi, but a stroll through Samcheong-dong village to the stations would be a nice experience!
